Contentder

Introduction

Contentder Nepal is a web development and digital marketing company based in Nepal. They offer services such as website development, e-commerce solutions, search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, and content creation. Contentder Nepal also provides custom software development and mobile app development services. Their aim is to help businesses establish a strong online presence and grow their digital reach.

Role
  • Template Creator
  • Web UI Design
  • Responsive Web Design
Understanding the Problem

At the inception of Contentder, there were no templates available to showcase to clients, and the company faced challenges in creating templates due to their inflexible approach.Additionally, Contentder encountered various issues while creating responsive designs, which added to the challenges of creating templates for clients. These challenges required the company to adapt and improve their design processes to better meet the needs of their clients.

Solution

As a template creator at Contentder was to design and develop visually appealing templates for various industries, including schools, travel, beauty, and e-commerce. This involved selecting appropriate fonts, color schemes, and images, and creating user-friendly layouts to ensure that the templates were easy to navigate. As a part of my responsibilities, I also ensured that the templates I created were responsive and could adapt to different screen sizes and devices. In addition, I was responsible for testing the accessibility of the templates to ensure that they were compliant with accessibility standards and accessible to individuals with disabilities. Overall, my goal was to create high-quality templates that met the needs and requirements of our clients.
