Neema Academy

Introduction

NeemaAcademy Nepal is a leading online learning platform that provides quality education to students in Nepal. They offer a wide range of courses, including IT, management, entrepreneurship, and language learning, among others. The platform is accessible and user-friendly, with features such as live classes, interactive quizzes, and personalized feedback.

Role
  • UX Research
  • Web UI Design
  • Mobile UI Design
  • Responsive Web Design
Research & Analysis

The principal task was to understand and address the issue. Neema academy was divided into two parts Learner and Instructor. To improvise the user experience for both users, it is neccesary to understand the need of the users.

I conducted user research and analysis. I started with creating user personas to represent the target audience. I interviewed students and professionals who had taken courses from Neema Academy to gain insight into their experiences and expectations.

The primary objective of conducting user research and analysis was to gain insights into the following factors:

  • What are the main pain points of the current solution?
  • How can I figure out solutions to those issues?
  • How can I reveal what—or which parts, or features—are the most important for users?
Problem

Based on the research, I identified the following pain points:

  • The existing website was outdated and difficult to navigate.
  • The mobile app had limited features and was not user-friendly.
  • The course details were not well-organized, making it hard to find relevant information.
  • It is difficult for first time users to trust the platform to pay for a course.
  • Existing users don’t generally search on the same site for their next course.
  • Learner had issue in following and understanding the course.
  • Instructor were having difficulty in controlling the content of the course they created.
  • Instructors were having trouble managing their learners.
User Persona
Rohan Sharma
15 yrs | Male | Student(Class 10) School: Bhawani Secondary School
Background

Maya is a 10th grade student studying in Shree Bhawani Secondary School, located in a rural area of Nepal. She comes from a middle-class family and has limited access to technology at home. Her parents value education and have encouraged her to study hard and achieve good grades. Maya is a diligent student who is passionate about learning new things.

Goals
  • To excel in her studies and achieve good grades
  • To prepare for upcoming exams
  • To stay organized and manage her schedule effectively
  • To access study materials and resources easily
  • To communicate with her classmates and teachers
Challenges
  • Limited access to technology at home
  • Slow internet connection
  • Limited availability of study materials and resources
  • Difficulty in understanding complex concepts in certain subjects
Behaviors
  • Uses her smartphone to access the internet and study materials
  • Prefers visual aids and interactive learning materials
  • Takes handwritten notes in class and revises them regularly
  • Participates actively in class discussions and group activities
  • Seeks help from teachers and classmates when faced with difficulties
Pain Points
  • Slow loading times for online resources
  • Difficulty in accessing study materials due to limited availability
  • Inability to communicate effectively with classmates and teachers outside of school hours
  • Difficulty in understanding complex concepts in certain subjects
Conclusion

By understanding the needs, behaviors, and pain points of Maya, the design team can create a student-centric interface that addresses his challenges and supports his goals.

Anish Shrestha
30 yrs | Male | Math Teacher School: Bhawani Secondary School
Background

Anish has been teaching science to grade 10 students for the past 5 years. He is passionate about his subject and wants his students to develop a love for science. He believes that science is an essential subject for students to learn, as it can help them understand the world around them and make informed decisions in the future. Anish is known for his engaging teaching style and his ability to simplify complex concepts for his students. He is always looking for new teaching strategies and resources to enhance histeaching.

Goals
  • To effectively communicate science concepts to his students and ensure they understand the material.
  • To make science interesting and engaging for his students.
  • To track his students' progress and provide feedback to help them improve.
  • To save time in lesson planning and grading, so he can focus on teaching and professional development
Challenges
  • Anish faces challenges in managing the large number of students in his class and giving individual attention to each one.
  • He has to create lesson plans and assessments that align with the curriculum and also cater to the different learning styles and abilities of his students.
  • He has to make sure that the students are engaged and interested in the subject to prevent them from losing focus and motivation.
Behaviors
  • Anish is a passionate teacher who loves to make his classes engaging and interactive.
  • He believes in teaching students with practical examples and experiments.
  • He is tech-savvy and enjoys exploring new educational tools and apps to make his teaching more effective.
  • He is a good communicator and encourages students to ask questions and clarify their doubts.
Pain Points
  • Limited time to prepare lesson plans and create teaching materials.
  • Difficulty in keeping track of student progress and identifying areas where students need extra help.
  • Limited resources to provide engaging and interactive learning experiences for her students.
  • Difficulty in providing individualized attention to each student in a class with many students
Conclusion

Based on the user persona of Anish, it is clear that the Neema Academy application should have features that cater to different learning styles and abilities of students. The application should provide resources that can help Mr. Shrestha create engaging lesson plans and assessments. The application should have features that encourage student-teacher interaction and allow Anish to provide individual attention to students who need it.

Design Process

Once I had a better understanding of the user needs, I started the design process.

  • Wireframes: I created wireframes of the website and mobile app using Figma. I started with low-fidelity wireframes to get the basic layout and structure in place. Once the layout was finalized, I moved on to high-fidelity wireframes that included visual design elements.
  • Visual Design: Using the wireframes as a guide, I created a visual design that reflected the branding of Neema Academy. I used a color palette that was consistent with their existing brand identity and created a style guide to ensure consistency throughout the design.
  • Front-end Development: After finalizing the visual design, I worked on the front-end development of the website and guided developer for mobile app. I used HTML, CSS to create responsive designs that worked well on all devices for web application.
  • Usability Testing: To ensure that the design was user-friendly, I conducted usability testing with a group of students and professionals. I used their feedback to make improvements to the design.
Solution

The goal was to reduce the friction users experienced and improve the product (web and mobile app) with new features. While we were redesigning the product, we had to add new features to the platform. Shipping features to the old platform and designing some new functionalities for the upcoming interface.

Added Key Features
  • The redesigned website and mobile app made it easy for users to find the information they needed. The navigation was intuitive and easy to use.
  • Added preview section for instructors after they created content of the course.
  • The course section was redesigned to make it easy to find courses. The information was organized in a clear and concise manner, making it easy to understand.
  • Interactive features like games, quiz and live test were implemented in course to make it engaging for learner.
  • Assignmnet section was added and instructors could remark the assignments submitted by learner.
  • Learner could added notes in content in form of text, image, audio, video and link for easier understanding.
Conclusion

The redesign of the Neema Academy website and mobile app provided a better user experience and increased user engagement. The new design was well-organized and easy to navigate, making it easy for users to find the information they needed. The addition of new features, such as course preview, interactive session, course reminders, increased user engagement and made it more convenient for users to access course materials. Overall, the redesign helped Neema Academy to achieve its goal of providing high-quality education to students.
