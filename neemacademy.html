Neema Academy

Web and UI/UX Designer

Introduction

NeemaAcademy Nepal is a leading online learning platform that provides quality education to students in Nepal. They offer a wide range of courses, including IT, management, entrepreneurship, and language learning, among others. The platform is accessible and user-friendly, with features such as live classes, interactive quizzes, and personalized feedback.

Problem

Learners had issues in following and understanding the course, instructors were having trouble managing their leaners. the course previews were unstructured in many cases, and there was a lack of interactive sessions for quizzes, assignments, articles and puzzles for learners. The user flow was also found to be inconsistent.

Goal

The goal was to reduce the friction users experienced and improve the product with new features. Redesigning the product, along side adding new features to the platform for both learner and instructors. Redesigning and integrating the landing page and planning the structure of the website. Need to create content grid system where instructure can enter the content in freely. Designing some new functionalities for the upcoming interface.
