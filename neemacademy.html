Neema Academy

Introduction

NeemaAcademy Nepal is a leading online learning platform that provides quality education to students in Nepal. They offer a wide range of courses, including IT, management, entrepreneurship, and language learning, among others. The platform is accessible and user-friendly, with features such as live classes, interactive quizzes, and personalized feedback.

Role
  • UX Research
  • Web UI Design
  • Mobile UI Design
  • Responsive Web Design
Understanding the Problem

The principal task was to understand and address the issue. Neema academy was divided into two parts Learner and Instructor. The job was to improvise user experience for both users. The principal issue was for the learner to have a clear understanding of the structure of their course.

  • What are the main pain points of the current solution?
  • How can I figure out solutions to those issues?
  • How can I reveal what—or which parts, or features—are the most important for users?
Problem
  • It is difficult for first time users to trust the platform to pay for a course.
  • Existing users don’t generally search on the same site for their next course.
  • Learner had issue in following and understanding the course.
  • Instructor were having difficulty in controlling the content of the course they created.
  • Instructors were having trouble managing their learners.
Solution

The goal was to reduce the friction users experienced and improve the product (web and mobile app) with new features. While we were redesigning the product, we had to add new features to the platform. Shipping features to the old platform and designing some new functionalities for the upcoming interface.

Added Features
  • Added preview section for instructors after they created content of the course.
  • Introduces multiple games for learner.
  • Implemented quiz and live test.
  • Assignmnet section was added and instructors could remark the assignments submitted by learner.
  • Learner could added notes in content in form of text, image, audio, video and link for easier understanding.
