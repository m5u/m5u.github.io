SageFlick

Introduction

Sageflick Nepal is a digital marketing and web development company that offers a range of services, including a theater management system called "BoxOffice Theatre Management System". This system helps theater managers streamline their operations, including ticket sales, seat reservation, and event management, while providing an enhanced experience for customers. In addition to the theater management system, Sageflick Nepal offers various other digital marketing and web development services to help businesses achieve their online goals.

Role
  • Web UI Design
  • Template Creater
  • Responsive Web Design
Problem

The ticketing system was experiencing issues on various screen devices, making it difficult for some users to use. Templates were not readily available, and the overall design of client projects tended to be similar. In addition, some of the previously implemented features were not functioning properly.

Solution

My task was to maintain the website's functionality and ensure that it was up-to-date. I added new features to the theater management system, and also created templates for client projects. Additionally, I was responsible for ensuring that the entire ticketing system of the project was seamless and easy to use. My goal was to provide a high-quality experience for both the theater managers and their customers.
